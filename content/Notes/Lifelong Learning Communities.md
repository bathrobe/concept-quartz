Communities can thrive by embedding continuous, low-intensity education into daily life.

- A 10% learning model integrates education seamlessly with social life, making it less burdensome and more sustainable.
- Socially available learning opportunities build accessible pathways for skill development and personal growth.
- A focus on constant learning fosters intellectual engagement and community resilience.
