The rise of AI and digital tools is shifting education from institutional control to self-directed learning, potentially making traditional educational structures obsolete.

- Technology enables personalized learning paths that can be more effective than standardized education.
- Self-directed learning becomes more powerful as tools for knowledge access and processing improve.
- This shift challenges traditional educational institutions to adapt or become irrelevant.
