Building communities within existing legal systems enables rapid implementation without the burden of sovereignty.

- Using established frameworks provides immediate access to infrastructure, legal protections, and administrative support.
- By avoiding sovereign-level challenges, communities can focus on cultivating cultural and social bonds.
- This approach allows iterative experimentation while retaining the flexibility to adapt and grow organically.
