Attempts to create sovereign spaces always require engagement with existing regulatory frameworks and power structures.

- Companies seeking property rights in space first needed state protection through legislation like the 2015 Space Act.
- Alternative governance projects like Próspera rely on existing state frameworks for their authority.
- Even space colonization requires working within Earth's legal and regulatory systems rather than escaping them.
