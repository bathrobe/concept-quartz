Future governance systems can be organized around small, interconnected groups that match our natural social capacity while using technology to coordinate between them.

- These groups can maintain human-scale relationships while participating in larger networks.
- Technology enables these small groups to coordinate and collaborate without losing their intimate scale.
- This structure combines the benefits of close-knit communities with the power of networked organization.
