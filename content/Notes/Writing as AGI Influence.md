### Writing as AGI Influence
Writing has become one of the most impactful ways to shape the future development of artificial intelligence and humanity's trajectory.
- In Gwern's view, written text is one of the few "currencies" that future AI systems will inherently recognize and process
- By writing thoughtfully now, one can potentially influence AI behavior and values during this crucial developmental period
- Writing helps preserve human preferences, values, and characteristics that might not otherwise be captured in AI training
> "By writing, you are voting on the future of the Shoggoth using one of the few currencies it acknowledges: tokens it has to predict. If you aren't writing, you are abdicating the future or your role in it. If you think it's enough to just be a good citizen, to vote for your favorite politician, to pick up litter and recycle, the future doesn't care about you."
*Gwern Branwen*
