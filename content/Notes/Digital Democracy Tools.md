Modern technology enables new forms of democratic participation and consensus-building that transcend traditional limitations of time and space.

- Tools like Polis demonstrate how machine learning can help identify areas of agreement and foster productive dialogue among large groups.
- Digital democracy platforms can help bridge divides by connecting people with similar views across traditional boundaries.
- These tools can operate at both local and global scales, enabling new forms of collective decision-making.
