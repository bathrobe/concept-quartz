Modern lifestyles will blend education and work into an iterative, lifelong process.

- As lifespans increase, multiple learning and working phases will replace linear career paths.
- The fusion of study and work will become a necessity in rapidly changing technological landscapes.
- Communities can support this shift by providing spaces and frameworks for continuous skill development.
