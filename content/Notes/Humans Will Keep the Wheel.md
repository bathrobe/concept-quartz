Companies will automate from the bottom up, keeping humans in charge of high-level strategy while AI handles execution.

- AI will first replace routine tasks before gradually moving up the organizational hierarchy.
- Human executives will maintain control over vision and long-term planning decisions.
- This hybrid approach may prove more successful than fully automated organizations due to human strategic thinking abilities.
