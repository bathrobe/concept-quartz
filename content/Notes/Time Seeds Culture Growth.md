Extended gatherings enable both rapid experimentation and deep cultural development.

- The duration of community interaction determines whether innovations can be properly tested and refined.
- Longer stays transform gatherings from events into lifestyles, enabling organic cultural evolution.
- Time allows for iterative development of both social practices and technical systems.
