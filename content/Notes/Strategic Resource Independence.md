Access to and control of critical resources within sovereign territory is fundamental to maintaining true strategic independence.
- Nations must balance environmental concerns with the strategic necessity of domestic resource production.
- Critical mineral processing capabilities are as important as the raw materials themselves.
- Resource independence should extend beyond direct territory to include reliable allied sources.
