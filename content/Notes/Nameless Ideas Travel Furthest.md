Anonymous writing allows ideas to be judged purely on their merits without the baggage of identity or status.

- Readers must engage with the content rather than dismissing it based on assumptions about the author.
- Anonymity provides protection for exploring controversial or unconventional ideas.
- The removal of identity markers forces deeper engagement with the actual arguments presented.
