### The 10% University Life
Life should maintain a constant, low-intensity learning environment rather than concentrated periods of intense study.
- A continuous learning approach at 10% intensity creates more sustainable knowledge acquisition than traditional intensive education periods.
- The integration of learning into daily social activities makes education a natural part of community life.
- This model provides more accessible and less intimidating pathways to continuous personal development.
> "Imagine going through your entire life like your living experience for your entire life being a University at 10% intensity, right? So not a university 100% intensity but keep it at ten percent intensity, have very easy and very socially available opportunities to constantly keep learning new things. If you really concentrate and really try to create network effects around specific kinds of people, you don't even need 30,000 - even three thousand is going to be enough."
*Vitalik Buterin*
