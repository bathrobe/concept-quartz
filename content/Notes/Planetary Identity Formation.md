The development of planetary-scale institutions must precede and can catalyze the formation of planetary identity and consciousness.
- New governance structures can actively shape how people understand their place in the world and their relationships to others.
- The European Union demonstrates how shared institutions can gradually foster new forms of identity and belonging.
- Creating planetary institutions could help develop a sense of planetary citizenship and shared destiny.
