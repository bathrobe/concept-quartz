Claims of sovereignty or independence must be preceded by development of practical capabilities and infrastructure.

- Space resource companies focused on securing property rights before developing technical capabilities and failed.
- Real governance power requires building practical infrastructure rather than just regulatory frameworks.
- Failed alternative governance projects often prioritize legal structures over operational capability.
