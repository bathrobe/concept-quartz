Extreme environments necessitate codified systems of mutual assistance and cooperation between potential competitors.

- The Outer Space Treaty of 1967 legally requires astronauts to help each other regardless of nationality.
- Hostile environments make competition-based systems unsustainable without underlying cooperative frameworks.
- Technical standards and interoperability become essential governance concerns when survival depends on cooperation.
