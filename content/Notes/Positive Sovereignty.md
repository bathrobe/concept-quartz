Sovereignty can be reimagined as the freedom to achieve collective goals rather than just freedom from external interference.
- Traditional sovereignty focused on protecting states from each other, but modern challenges require collective action.
- A positive conception of sovereignty emphasizes building capabilities through cooperation rather than isolation.
- This reframing could help overcome resistance to necessary planetary-scale coordination.
