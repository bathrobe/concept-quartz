### Bottom-Up AI Integration 
The transition to AI-augmented organizations will happen from the bottom up, preserving human strategic vision at the top while automating tactical execution.
- Initial automation will focus on routine tasks and gradually work upward through organizational hierarchies
- Human executives will retain control of long-term vision and strategic decision-making
- This model may outcompete fully automated organizations due to humans' superior long-term planning capabilities
> "All of the pressures are to go bottom-up. From existing things, it's just much more palatable in every way to start at the bottom and replace there and work your way up, to eventually where you just have human executives overseeing a firm of AIs."
*Gwern Branwen*
