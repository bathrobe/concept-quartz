A nation's true strategic independence depends on its ability to maintain critical industrial production during global supply chain disruptions.
- Modern military capabilities require complex supply chains that must be secured and duplicated for resilience.
- Environmental regulations should be balanced against the urgent need for domestic industrial capacity.
- Rapid industrial mobilization capabilities are as crucial as high-tech advantages in modern strategic competition.
