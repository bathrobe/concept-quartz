Written content will shape future AI systems because it forms the basis of their training data and understanding of human values.

- Writing is one of the few direct ways humans can influence how AI systems develop their understanding of the world.
- Content created now will help determine what values and preferences get encoded into future AI systems.
- Unlike voting or activism, writing creates lasting data points that AI systems must process and learn from.
