Communities grounded in education naturally foster strong bonds and shared goals.

- Educational structures provide purpose and cohesion, giving community members shared values and motivations.
- Physical and social environments amplify learning outcomes compared to purely online platforms.
- Positive network effects from collaborative learning strengthen the community over time.
