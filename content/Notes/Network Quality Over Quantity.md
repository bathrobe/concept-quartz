Smaller, focused communities benefit more from quality members than sheer numbers.

- Overly large networks risk introducing actors who detract from the collective value.
- Curated, intentional memberships create environments where shared values and goals drive growth.
- Communities should aim to optimize for alignment and collaboration over indiscriminate expansion.
