### The Power of Anonymity
Anonymity enables ideas to be evaluated on their merits rather than through preconceived notions about their source.
- Prevents social status and identity from biasing the reception of ideas
- Protects individuals from retaliation while allowing them to explore controversial topics
- Creates space for ideas to be heard without immediate dismissal based on the messenger
> "The most underrated benefit of anonymity is that people don't project onto you as much. They can't slot you into any particular niche or identity and write you off in advance. They have to at least read you a little bit to even begin to dismiss you."
*Gwern Branwen*
