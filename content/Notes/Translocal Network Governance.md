Effective governance networks can form between geographically distant regions that face similar challenges, transcending traditional territorial boundaries.
- Cities and regions with similar ecological challenges can form direct governance partnerships, like Jakarta and Rotterdam collaborating on water management.
- These networks prioritize practical problem-solving over traditional diplomatic relations or geographic proximity.
- This model allows for rapid sharing of expertise and resources between areas facing similar challenges, regardless of national borders.
