### Working Within Nations
Building new communities inside existing countries provides most benefits while avoiding sovereign-level complications.
- Creating communities within existing legal frameworks allows for faster implementation and fewer regulatory hurdles.
- Working with established systems enables communities to focus on building social structures rather than solving sovereignty challenges.
- The approach allows for experimentation while maintaining legal protections and infrastructure access.
> "Working with existing countries is just something that's gonna realistically make sense for projects going into the future for a while. One of the ways to kind of break down a country is that there is the social network and there are the legal and jurisdictional properties, and possibly a third one is like physical location properties."
*Vitalik Buterin*
