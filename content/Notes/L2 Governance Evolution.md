New governance structures can be built as overlays on existing systems without requiring the dismantling of current institutions.

- This approach mirrors natural evolution, where new capabilities develop on top of existing structures rather than replacing them.
- The strategy allows for experimentation with new forms of governance without direct confrontation with existing power structures.
- This layered approach enables parallel development of alternative systems while maintaining stability.
