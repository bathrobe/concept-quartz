Different planetary challenges require governance responses at different scales, with authority distributed according to the nature of the problem.
- Global issues like carbon emissions require planetary-level coordination and enforcement.
- Implementation of global standards often works best at the national level where it intersects with economic policy.
- Local adaptation strategies should be developed and managed at the regional or city level where specific conditions can be addressed.
