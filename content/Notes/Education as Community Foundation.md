### Education as Community Foundation
Education naturally creates the social bonds and positive network effects needed for new communities to thrive.
- Learning environments create strong social bonds that online-only education platforms have failed to replicate effectively.
- Educational communities provide a clear organizational structure that helps maintain cohesion and purpose.
- The shared pursuit of knowledge creates natural network effects that strengthen community ties over time.
> "Learning and motivation are very social right and being really successful in those things actually depends on having the right social environment. This is one of the key reasons why Coursera and the various MOOCs have failed to completely replace Stanford. You need to be intentional about the social environment and have friends that help you bring out the virtues that you want to see in yourself."
*Vitalik Buterin*
