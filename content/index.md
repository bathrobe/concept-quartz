This is the Concept government research vault.

- This vault extracts interesting ideas from source materials and turns them into notes using LLMs.
- Source material useful for Concept research can be found in the Sources folder. The extracted ideas are in the Notes folder.
- The notes are managed in Obsidian and (mostly) written by Claude Desktop using an [MCP server](https://github.com/MarkusPfundstein/mcp-obsidian).
- All Concept members can add sources to the vault. The vault is managed by Joe ([@joetforhire](https://x.com/joetforhire) on Twitter).
